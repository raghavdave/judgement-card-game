♠ How to Play — Judgement

💡 Prediction

After cards are dealt, each player predicts how many tricks they will win (0 to cards dealt this round). Players predict in turn order. The last player cannot make the total predictions equal the number of tricks available.

🃏 Trick Rules

  • The lead player plays any card.
  • Other players must follow the lead suit if they have it.
  • If you can't follow suit, you may cut (play trump) or fuse (play any other card).
  • In a No Trump round, cutting is not allowed.

🏆 Trick Winner

  • If trump cards were played → highest trump wins.
  • If no trump → highest lead-suit card wins.
  • Card rank (high→low): A K Q J 10 9 8 7 6 5 4 3 2

💲 Scoring

  • Exact prediction: +11 × predicted + 10
  • Wrong prediction: −11 × predicted − 1

Highest cumulative score at the end of all rounds wins.
